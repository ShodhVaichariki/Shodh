Ethical Policy & Author Guidelines

 

Guideline for Publication in “SHODH VAICHARIKI”:

Ethical Policy:

 

  • 1.  Papers submitted for publication should be solely original and unpublished.
  • 2.  The corresponding author must write covering letter along with declaration certificate regarding plagiarism.
  • 3.  All individuals listed as author or co-authors must made substantial contributions and approve the final version of the article/research paper to be published.
  • 4.  The corresponding author must confirm that the author list is complete, that all co-authors have agreed to be included in the author list, and that all co-authors have read and agreed to submit the manuscript.
  • 5.  The contribution of other individuals/organizations or sources should be recognized as per law.
  • 6.  Authors are responsible for any copyright clearance, factual inaccuracies and opinion expressed in their paper.
  • 7.  Corresponding author should write “Conflict of Interest Statement”. Author/authors must disclose all conflicts of interest in the cover letter of their submission for consideration by the Journal.
  • 8.  After getting the manuscript, the acknowledgement will be sent to the corresponding author through email which does not guarantee for publication.
  • 9.  The editorial board will review article/research paper and the approved/recommended articles/ research papers will be sent to the reviewers/ referees for critical review of the paper.
  • 10.  All the papers will be sent to the referees for their suggestions and modifications. The corresponding author will be intimated for the same if any.
  • 11.  The acceptance letter will be issued after getting clearance from reviewer/reviewers/ referees.
  • 12.  The views expressed in the articles/research papers are the views of author/authors. It is not essential for editorial board members to be in agreement or disagreement. The sole responsibilities of the views expressed in article/paper are of the author/authors.
  • 13.  All decisions regarding members of Advisory board, Editorial board, Review board, Referee will rest with Editor-in–Chief.

 

Publication Policy:

Manuscript Submission:

  • 1.  Manuscript must be original and typed in double space on the one side of paper (A-4) and have a sufficient margin in Times New Roman Font size 12 for English and Kruti Dev 10 Font size 14 for Hindi.
  • 2.  Script should be checked before submission as there is not provision of sending proof.
  • 3.  It must include Abstract, Introduction, Methods, Results and References.
  • 4.  Manuscripts should be typed on one side of the paper Manuscripts should be in Microsoft Word Program.
  • 5.  Maximum length of article/research paper should not exceed 3000 words including an abstract of 250 words.
  • 6.  All the manuscripts should be in two copies and in CD/e-mail : shodhvaichariki@gmail.com or drvpandey70@gmail.com
  • 7.  Abstract should contain brief background/introduction, method, major finding/ issues discussed in article/paper and key words.
  • 8.  Papers must be accompanied with brief bio-data, email id and mobile number for future reference.
  • 9.  All diagrams, figures and tables should be in black and white only.
  • 10.  Authors are solely responsible for the factual accuracy of their contribution.
  • 11.  Proper biographical references must be given alphabetically in APA style at the end of the text or the following format may be used-
